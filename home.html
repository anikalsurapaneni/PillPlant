Our Scanner + Product
PILL-PLANT is a product that works side-by-side with an application that can either scan the medication itself or the barcode and tells the user if they can purify/dilute the pharmaceuticals in order for it to be used as liquid plant fertilizer.

Safely dispose of your pharmaceuticals.

THE PROBLEM

Once a pharmaceutical expires, many people improperly dispose of it in ways like flushing it down the toilet, throwing it in the trash, or burning it. This can cause the pharmaceutical to leach into water systems, impact aquatic species (there are endocrine disrupting compounds), contaminate food and water systems, or can get into the wrong hands. Pharmaceuticals have so many nutrients such as Iron, Calcium, and Magnesium that can be utilized as fertilizer for soil enrichment.

WHY PILLPLANT?

Our system can either scan the barcode of the pharmaceutical container or it can scan the pharmaceutical itself and use ML to identify what it is, provide information about it, show the active ingredients, and its molecular structure. When it is connected to our device, the substance goes through a process which you can find more about here.
